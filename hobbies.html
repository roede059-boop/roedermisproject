My Hobbies

These hobbies are important to me because they make up who I am!

Crafting

My mom has always said that I got bitten by the creative bug when I was young. I have always loved creating, whether I am making jewelry, painting, scrapbooking, or embroidering! Picking up a paintbrush and getting to work on a creative project always soothes me because it means I can work on something that will bring me joy. A personal story I have with this hobby is that my best friend and I sold crafts at the UMD art sale last spring!

Dancing

I started Irish dancing when I was eight years old. Since then, I have loved the creativity it has given me. I am Irish, so it has given me a way to connect with my roots. I have traveled to many different states in order to compete. My dream is to one day go to Ireland and dance in the World's competition! My biggest achievement was that I got the solo in a team dance that won the Creative Freestyle dance competition.

Planting

I have always had a soft spot for plants because they are so delicate, and the fact that they are alive amazes me. I am a person who loves to take care of others, so having plants helps me fill that need. I also think they are beautiful because they can grow in various settings and still thrive. The picture below shows my shamrocks, which I have had for three years. They are my favorite because I got them at my last dance performance from my best friends.

Tennis

I have been playing tennis since seventh grade. My favorite type of game is singles, six games, two out of three sets wins. I love to hit back and forth with my sister, and I have a strong forehand. I enjoy doubles, but I like it when I have full control of the court and can play out the point as I see fit. I once played a game that lasted three hours!
