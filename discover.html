Discover UMD

The University of Minnesota Duluth is a great place to explore!

Why did I choose UMD?

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because my sister, whom I am very close to, attended UMD to further expand her education. I came up here to visit her and fell in love with the university and the surrounding area, which made me decide to go here. I also received scholarships based on my field of study, which helped my decision to commit to UMD.

What I like about Duluth and the Surrounding Area

One of the things I love about Duluth is that it is a medium-sized university. I toured other schools, but they were either too small, having only a grocery store and a gas station around them, or way too big, having over 100,000 students. I believe this university is right in the middle because there is a lot to explore, but not too many students to crowd it.

Another thing I like about Duluth is the surrounding nature. I live in a house only a couple of minutes away from campus, and we constantly have deer in our backyard. I especially love it when it is birthing season, and I get to see the fawns! There is one family of deer in particular that always visits us and has even let us get very close to them. I love animals, so having the chance to watch them so close to my house is very exciting for me!

Personal highlights and experiences in Duluth!

  • • Getting to see the northern lights
  • • Living with my best friends
  • • Exploring the nature around me
  • • Presenting a project for UMD professors and the chancellor
  • • Getting to be a part of Bulldog Yoga and Pickleball
  • • Meeting new friends because of classes
